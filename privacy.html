Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

Overview

SubKiller by Alvento Ltd is committed to protecting your privacy. This tool processes your financial data entirely on your device - we never receive, store, or transmit your bank statements or transaction data to our servers.

Data Processing

Client-Side Only: All CSV, Excel, and PDF processing happens in your browser using JavaScript. Your financial data never leaves your device.

No Account Required: We don't require registration, logins, or personal information to use the core features.

File Storage: Uploaded files are processed in your browser's memory and automatically discarded when you close the page.

Optional Data Collection

Email Capture: If you opt-in to receive our subscription cancellation kit, we collect your email address to send you the requested materials and occasional money-saving tips. You can unsubscribe at any time.

License Keys: Pro license keys are stored locally in your browser to maintain your subscription status.

Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand usage patterns and improve the service. This includes anonymized data about page views and feature usage.

Third-Party Services

Google AdSense: We display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and collect information to show relevant ads based on your interests. Google may also use information from your visits to this and other websites to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings. Google Privacy Policy

Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors. Google Analytics Privacy Policy

Amazon Associates: We are a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for us to earn fees by linking to Amazon and affiliated sites. When you click on Amazon links and make purchases, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Stripe: Payment processing for Pro licenses is handled by Stripe. Stripe Privacy Policy

Cookies and Local Storage

Local Storage: We use browser local storage to:

  • Remember your Pro license status
  • Save your detection settings preferences
  • Provide basic functionality

Third-Party Cookies: Google AdSense may use cookies to:

  • Show you relevant advertisements
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent you from seeing the same ads repeatedly

Cookie Control: You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of advertisements and analytics. To opt out of Google's personalized ads, visit Google Ad Settings.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your financial data, there's no server-side data to secure. Your bank statements and transaction data remain entirely under your control on your device.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use the service without providing any personal information
  • Request removal of your email from our mailing list
  • Clear local data by clearing your browser storage
  • Contact us with privacy concerns
