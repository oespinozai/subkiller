The Problem We Solve

The average person has 12-15 active subscriptions but only remembers 7-9 of them. Hidden subscription creep costs families hundreds of pounds annually through:

  • Free trials that auto-convert to paid plans
  • Services that increase prices without clear notification
  • Multiple subscriptions for similar services
  • Forgotten subscriptions from old email addresses
  • Services that make cancellation deliberately difficult
